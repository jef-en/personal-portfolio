    01. Introduction.

    Project Overview.

    Paw Hub is a Flutter-based app enhancing pet care in Naga City, Philippines. It centralizes vet shops and offers four features: pet medical records, appointment booking, a pet product marketplace, and updates on local pet events.

    Responsibilities.

    • UI/UX Designer
    • Front End Developer

    Project Duration.

    Aug 2023 - Dec 2023 (5 months)

    Tools.

    • Android Studio
    • Figma

    02. Problem Statement.

    Identifying the Problem.

    Pet owners in Naga City struggled with the lack of a centralized platform for veterinary services, making it challenging to find and choose suitable options. This inefficiency caused frustration as they relied on scattered sources to locate and evaluate services.

    Research Findings.

    Through preliminary research and user interviews, we identified several key issues:

    • Fragmented Information: Pet owners had to gather information from multiple sources, including scattered online listings and physical directories.
    • Accessibility Issues: Without a centralised platform, users had trouble finding the most convenient and nearest veterinary services.
    • Lack of Comparative Data: Users had no easy way to compare services, reviews, or prices for veterinary care in their area.

    03. Goals.

    Primary Goals.

    Paw Hub aims to centralize veterinary clinics in Naga City with a mobile app, making it easier for pet owners to access services, compare prices, and store their pets' health records digitally.
