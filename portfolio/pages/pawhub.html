    01. Introduction.

    Project Overview.

    Paw Hub is a mobile startup application that is made using Flutter which aims to enhance pet care and veterinary services in Naga City, Philippines by centralising the veterinary shops in the area. The application boasts four main features: Pet Record - which provides a platform for vets and pet owners to access the pet’s data, including vaccination records and medical history, Booking Vet Services - which enables pet owners to view partner clinic schedules and schedule appointments at their preferred time and date, Marketplace - which offers a diverse range of pet-related products for sale, catering to the needs of pet owners and enthusiasts, and Events - which displays upcoming pet-related events (e.g., Free Neuter and Spay), ensuring users stay informed and can attend relevant events.

    02. Problem Statement.

    Identifying the Problem.

    Pet owners in Naga City faced significant challenges due to the lack of centralised veterinary services. The absence of a unified platform made it difficult for them to find and choose nearby veterinary services that met their needs. This lack of centralization led to inefficiencies and frustrations, as users had to search through various sources to locate and evaluate veterinary options.

    Research Findings.

    Through preliminary research and user interviews, we identified several key issues:

    • Fragmented Information: Pet owners had to gather information from multiple sources, including scattered online listings and physical directories.
    • Accessibility Issues: Without a centralised platform, users had trouble finding the most convenient and nearest veterinary services.
    • Lack of Comparative Data: Users had limited means to compare services, reviews, and prices for veterinary care in their area.

    03. Goals.

    The primary goal of Paw Hub is to create a mobile application that centralises all veterinary shops and clinics in Naga City, making it easier for pet owners to access various veterinary services and compare prices. One of its key features is the ability to show pet owners the nearest veterinary services based on their location. Additionally, the app includes a digital medical record system, allowing pet owners to store their pets' health information in a centralised, digital format.

    04. Design Process.

    Colors and Fonts.

    Since the primary focus of our app is pet-related, we’ve designed the user experience to be vibrant and engaging by incorporating bright, fun colors. This not only reflects the playful and joyful nature of pets but also creates an inviting and friendly atmosphere for users. For the font, we’ve chosen Lexend, a typeface that enhances readability and accessibility, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable browsing experience for all users.

    User Flow.

    The user flow diagrams illustrate the seamless journey users take through the app, from start to finish. By mapping out key interactions and decision points, I was able to identify the most intuitive paths for users to follow, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience. These flows serve as a blueprint for both the design and development teams, highlighting critical screens, features, and transitions. The goal was to reduce friction, streamline navigation, and ensure that each action within the app feels logical and purposeful, guiding users effortlessly toward their objectives.

    Mid Fidelity Mockups.

    In this phase of the design process, I created mid-fidelity mockups to bring structure and functionality to the app's user interface. These mockups showcase the app's core layout, navigation, and interactions with more defined UI elements like buttons, text, and icons, while still allowing room for iteration. They offer a clearer sense of the user experience, helping to visualize the flow and overall design before refining the final details in high-fidelity prototypes.

    High Fidelity Mockups.

    High-fidelity mockups showcasing detailed and polished designs that closely mirror the final product. These mockups highlight user interface elements, interactions, and visual aesthetics to provide a realistic preview of the user experience.
