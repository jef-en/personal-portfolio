    01. Introduction.

    Project Overview.

    Paw Hub is a mobile startup application that is made using Figma and Flutter which aims to enhance pet care and veterinary services in Naga City, Philippines by centralising the veterinary shops in the area. The application boasts four main features: Pet Record - which provides a platform for vets and pet owners to access the petâ€™s data, including vaccination records and medical history, Booking Vet Services - which enables pet owners to view partner clinic schedules and schedule appointments at their preferred time and date, Marketplace - which offers a diverse range of pet-related products for sale, catering to the needs of pet owners and enthusiasts, and Events - which displays upcoming pet-related events (e.g., Free Neuter and Spay), ensuring users stay informed and can attend relevant events.
