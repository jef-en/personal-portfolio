02. Dr. Abels Adventure.

"Dr. Abel's Adventures" is an educational game for Grade 8 students at Camarines Sur National High School. It simplifies the prevention and control of communicable diseases like influenza, tuberculosis, and malaria through engaging gameplay. With appealing visuals, audio, and informative content, the game makes learning enjoyable, fostering awareness and proactive measures against disease transmission.
