04. Go Jeep

Go Jeep is a jeepney booking app created to address transportation challenges during the COVID-19 pandemic. It aims to provide a safer and more convenient experience for passengers and drivers through features like advance booking and contact tracing.

the.problem

life in the "new normal."

Operating jeepneys in the post-COVID "new normal" is challenging due to their crowded, shared nature, which increases virus transmission risks. Passengers face limited health screening and social distancing, making rides potentially unsafe.


However, stopping operations isn't viable, as drivers depend on daily earnings. The challenge is to ensure safe jeepney services that protect public health while supporting drivers' livelihoods

photo by: Neil Clark Ongchangco

the.goals

the innovation we need

Go Jeep aims to make jeepney transportation safer and more efficient in the post-COVID era. By offering features like advance booking and contact tracing, the app reduces crowding, minimizes physical contact, and supports health protocols. Passengers can book rides in advance for a safer travel experience, while the contact tracing feature helps manage potential virus exposure.

design.process

color and fonts

I think the color I picked here is too vibrant for a booking application. I should have chosen more neutral or monotone colors to make it easier on the eyes. As for the font, I chose a combination of Montserrat and Hind. I believe it fits the concept well since both fonts complement each other and are easy to read.

mid fidelity wireframes - final wireframes

We created mid-fidelity mockups first to ensure that the elements, layout, and overall structure aligned with the project goals before proceeding to high-fidelity designs.
